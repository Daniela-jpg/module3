The main parts of the chicken are: breast, thigh, drumstick and wings. They also form part of the heart, gizzards and liver, but these are usually sold separately from the chicken itself. Depending on the cut and the previous preparation, the meat pieces of such birds can be stored in a freezer, for 3 to 6 months. Chicken meat is surrounded by a skin, which is high in fat and cholesterol. For this reason, health and nutrition professionals recommend removing the skin from the chicken when it is eaten.
